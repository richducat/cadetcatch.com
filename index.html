FAQ

Questions families ask

How does the photo matching actually work?

CadetCatch uses facial-similarity search: it compares one clear photo of your cadet with faces in the indexed event-photo collection and ranks likely matches. It suggests — you decide. It never guarantees an identity or decides who is in a photo.

How much time does it really save?

A big photo drop can run to hundreds or thousands of images — and after Day One haircuts, every face looks alike. Instead of opening each one, you review a ranked shortlist. Most families go from a fresh gallery to saved finds in minutes.

What happens to the photo I upload?

Your cadet's profile stays on your device. The reference photo is sent securely to the search service only to find matches — never used for advertising or tracking. Details in our privacy policy.

What does it cost?

The app is free to download. A one-time photo check or single photo unlock is $1.99, and Family Monthly is $12.99/month with unlimited searches and family invites. Purchases go through Apple.

Can my spouse and family use it too?

Yes — Family Monthly includes spouse, family, and cadet access invites, delivered by email and redeemed with the invited address.

Is CadetCatch affiliated with the Academy?

No. CadetCatch is an independent family resource and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to USCGA, the U.S. Coast Guard, or DHS.
